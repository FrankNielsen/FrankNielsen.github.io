Curved Bregman divergence

Example: curved circle Euclidean divergence

Demo in maxima:
/* curved circle Euclidean (Bregman) divergence */
kill(all)$
BD(theta1,theta2):=(1/2)* ( (theta1[1]-theta2[1])**2  + (theta1[2]-theta2[2])**2);
theta(u):=[cos(u),sin(u)];
exu1:random(float(2*%pi));
exu2:random(float(2*%pi));
extheta1: theta(exu1);
extheta2: theta(exu2);
BD(extheta1,extheta2);
BDc(u1,u2):= (1/2)*( (cos(u1)-cos(u2))**2 + (sin(u1)-sin(u2))**2 );
BDc(exu1,exu2);
