Jensen-Shannon centroid

Consider the set of categorical distributions (normalized histograms) as a mixture family in information geometry. Jensen-Shannon divergence between two categorical distributions then amounts to a corresponding Jensen divergence. Uses the Convex-ConCave procedure to locally optimize the JS centroid.
