• f-divergences (including α-divergences, Jensen-Shannon divergence, total variation, etc):
    • On f-divergences between Cauchy distributions, arxiv:2101.12459

      f-divergences between Cauchy densities are always symmetric and expressed as a strictly increasing function of the chi-squared divergence. Report closed-form formula for the Kullback-Leibler divergence, Jensen-Shannon divergence, triangular divergence, Taneja divergence, Hellinger divergence, etc.

    • On the Chi Square and Higher-Order Chi Distances for Approximating f-Divergences (IEEE SPL 2014).
      On power chi expansions of f-divergences (arXiv:1903.05818)

      Taylor expansions of f-divergences using higher-order chi-squared divergences. Any other chi-squared divergence between densities of an exponential family is available in closed-form when the natural parameter space is affine (e.g., Poisson family, isotropic Gaussian family, von-Mises Fisher, etc.)
