List of Processing demo codes

  • AmariAlphaDivergenceBDPDE.pde : Implement and check that the alpha divergence extended to positive measures amount to a representation Bregman divergence or equivalently to dual representational Fenchel-Young divergences.

    see Nielsen, Frank, and Richard Nock. "The dual Voronoi diagrams with respect to representational Bregman divergences." 2009 Sixth International Symposium on Voronoi Diagrams. IEEE, 2009.

  • ExtendedAlpha2BetaDivergencePDE : Implement and check that alpha-divergences amount to representational Bregman divergences

    Dikmen, Onur, Zhirong Yang, and Erkki Oja. "Learning the information divergence." IEEE transactions on pattern analysis and machine intelligence 37.7 (2014): 1442-1454.
