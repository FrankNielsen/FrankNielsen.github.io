Quasi-arithmetic means

  • Cargo, G. T., and O. Shisha. "A metric space connected with generalized means." Journal of Approximation Theory 2.2 (1969): 207-222.

    Provide a distance between QAMs by considering equivalence classes of QAM generators, yields a metric space (Theorem 1). Distance is maximum of QAM absolute difference wrt to x inside hyperrectangle and weight in standard simplex. We can approximate m_h by m_poly such that distance is less or equal than eps by a polynomial with rational coefficients generator

    keywords: moduli space, Bernstein polynomials, canonical functions, upper bounds of distances
