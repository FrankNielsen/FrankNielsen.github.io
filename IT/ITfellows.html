IEEE fellow program Nomination forms

The newly elevated IEEE Fellows are announced shortly after the November IEEE Board of Directors meeting in the year preceding the official elevation to IEEE Fellow (November 19 November 24 November 2025). Elevation to IEEE Fellow is bestowed on 1 January.
