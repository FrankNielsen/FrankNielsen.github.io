  • Euclidean distance (equivalent to squared Euclidean Bregman divergence for the minimax optimization problem)
  • Mahalanobis distance (equivalent to squared Mahalanobis Bregman divergence for the minimax optimization problem)

  • Itakura-Saito divergence (IS)
  • Logistic (loss) divergence
  • Exponential (loss) divergence
  • Csiszar divergence (alpha=0.5)
